Our Story

The Apothecary was a dream realized by Raed and Caroline back in 2010. Inspired by traditional herbal medicines, they passion for tea began. Together they researched the healing properties of natural herbs and flowers to use as supplementation to modern mediciene. Raed, who worked as a Registered Nurse for many years, would often find himself writing tea recipies along with his patient's prescriptions. Caroline, who worked as a programmer for many years, became very interested in coffee as she would have to have several cups daily to survive the day. Eventually they decided to quit their day jobs and open the Apothecary Coffee & Tea House.

We at Apothecary Coffee & Tea House welcome everyone to join us as we eat, drink, & be merry.

We have free high-speed internet available for all our customers including our students, writers, and gamers. Every table has its own outlet and USB plugs for your devices. If you would like to reserve our private study room please ask for the sign-up sheet at the counter.

Our community library offers dozens of titles in several genres. We encourage our customers to leave a book and take a book so that our collection is always changing and offering something new. We also have a special bookshelf for young children featuring picture books, short chapter books, and also coloring books.

For our customers who would like to have a little fun, we have some free classic table-top games such as chess available to check out at the counter. Please remember to return all the pieces of the game to the counter so that we can sanitize and reset the game for others.
